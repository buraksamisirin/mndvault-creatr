Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

MndVault Creatr ("we", "our", or "us") provides tools for connecting supported social media accounts and publishing short-form video content. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following information:

  • Account identifiers from connected social media platforms
  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens
  • Video metadata such as title, caption, hashtags, and scheduled posting time
  • Basic usage and error logs needed to operate and troubleshoot the service
  • Contact information if you contact us directly

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Authenticate users and connect their social media accounts

  • Maintain account connection state
  • Monitor reliability, troubleshoot failures, and improve the service
  • Respond to support requests

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only as necessary with:

  • Supported social media platform APIs to perform requested publishing actions
  • Infrastructure or hosting providers used to operate the service
  • Legal authorities if required by law

4. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as necessary to provide the service, maintain security, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect stored information, including restricting access to sensitive credentials and minimizing exposure of tokens and private data.
